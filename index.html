Welcome to Graduation 2025

Welcome to the digital record of Victoria University's 2025 Graduation Ceremony. This historic event celebrated the achievements of over 1,200 graduates across various disciplines.

The ceremony was held on December 15, 2025, at the University Main Auditorium, presided over by the Chancellor, Dr. Michael Ntambi.
